Open Technology Sessions

Welcome SOFIC Attendees and Community Developers!

Mil-OSS of Central Florida is pleased to offer the 7th Annual Working Group two events bringing together military and civilian government employees, contractors, industry experts, and students to discuss defense-industry open source advocacy. In conjunction with our annual Hackathon as well as the Special Operations Forces Industry Conference (SOFIC).

All sessions are lead by national and local subject matter experts well versed in the unique aspects of implementing open technologies. This is a premiere event for anyone currently looking to apply the latest in open technologies to their organization and will also occur at SOFWERX.

Topics include but are not limited to:

  • Open Source Policy
  • 3rd Offset Strategy
  • Open Source Business Models In The DoD
