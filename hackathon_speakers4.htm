Red Hat Senior Solution Architect

Agile Application Development

Matt supports DoD customers implementing middleware and platform as a service (PaaS) solutions using Red Hat JBoss and OpenShift platforms. He advises enterprise as well as tactical programs across the major service branches on legacy modernization and innovation adoption for new start programs.

Ted Brunell
Red Hat Senior Solution Architect

Software Defined Everything

Ted works for the DoD Team and supports the Department Information Systems Agency. Prior to joining Red Hat, he worked for a large system integrator supporting the U.S. Government. Active in a local Linux Users Group, he enjoys talking about the use of open source technology and cloud computing to improve business and IT workflows.

Anthony Calamito
Federal CTO, Boundless Spatial

Hybrid GIS

Anthony helps guide the overall technical strategy and serve as a bridge between customers and engineering to ensure development tasks and enhancements are documented and properly meet customer requirements. He helps identify and qualify business development opportunities, as well as develop marketing materials in conjunction with overall marketing message framework.

Anthony is also a LocationTech Steering Committee member, an adjunct instructor at George Mason University, and an American Geographical Society Fellow. Anthony has a BS in Geography with a focus on GIS from The Pennsylvania State University.

Mark Swanson
Managing Director, Lane Five Ventures

3rd Offset Strategy

A technology entrepreneur, Mark has successfully started and grown six multi-million dollar companies. Most recently, Mark was the co-founder and CEO of Tampa-based, Telovations, an Inc. 500 Company which he sold to Bright House Networks. At Bright House, he served as Vice President of Strategic Initiatives. Mark started his career in the US Army where he commanded several military units including forming the first unit of Apache attack helicopters and also managed high-tech projects as a test pilot.

Mark’s industry recognition includes two Inc. 500 Awards, four Fast 50 Awards, Tampa Bay Entrepreneur of the Year, two Emmy Awards for digital media production, The New Media Invision Award, five Webbie Awards, New York City’s Champions of Technology Award and runner up in the Ernst & Young International Business Plan competition. He earned a degree in Computer Engineering from West Point and a MS in Technology Management from the Georgia Institute of Technology.

Jacob Redding
Accenture

New Wave of Open Source

Redding is open source developer and technologist that is passionate about Open Source. He has contributed to open source projects for over 15 years including leadership positions within the Drupal open source project. Jacob served as the Drupal Association's first Executive Director from 2010-2013; the non-profit dedicated to supporting the Drupal project. He currently works as an Open Source Manager and evangelist at Accenture helping Enterprise customers adopt strong open source governance policies and improve internal and external collaboration and contributions including guidance on developing their own open source project.
