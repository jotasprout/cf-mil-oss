Matt Zager
Red Hat Senior Solution Architect

Mr. Zager has been a Senior Solution Architect with Red Hat for 4 years supporting DoD customers implementing middleware and platform as a service (PaaS) solutions using Red Hat JBoss and OpenShift platforms. In this capacity he has advised both enterprise as well as tactical programs across the major service branches on legacy modernization as well as innovation adoption for new start programs. Prior to joining Red Hat Mr. Zager performed roles in software development, systems engineering and as a business unit chief technology officer over 17 years at a large DoD systems integrator.

Tim Zonca
Puppet Labs Director of Product Marketing

Mr. Zonca, Director of Product Marketing at Puppet Labs has nearly two decades of experience working with IT around security, scheduling, process automation, collaboration, DevOps and more at both large technology providers as well as start ups.

Ted Brunell
Red Hat Senior Solution Architect

Mr. Brunell is a Senior Solution Architect at Red Hat and works as a the Senior Architect for the DoD Team and supports the Department Information Systems Agency. Prior to joining Red Hat more than four ago, he worked for a large system integrator supporting the U.S. Government. A long-time Linux hobbyist, Ted is active in a local Linux Users Group and enjoys talking with others about the use of open source technology and cloud computing to improve business and IT workflows.
